Who We Are

An International Council for Cyber Intelligence Excellence.

The Cyber Threat Intelligence Council (CTIC) is a global professional body focused on advancing the discipline of cyber threat intelligence and security operations. We operate at the intersection of intelligence methodology, operational security practice, forensic investigation standards, and professional competency validation.

Our Vision

A More Structured Global Cyber Defense Community

We envision a global cybersecurity ecosystem where:

  • Intelligence professionals operate under defined ethical standards
  • Competency is measured against structured benchmarks
  • Institutions align with globally recognized frameworks
  • Cyber defense decisions are evidence-based and intelligence-driven
CTIC seeks to contribute to this ecosystem through governance, research, and professional development initiatives.
Governance & Integrity

Built on Accountability and Transparency

CTIC maintains a governance-driven structure designed to ensure:

  • Independent oversight
  • Examination integrity
  • Ethical compliance
  • Responsible data handling
  • Professional accountability
Our operations are guided by defined policies and structured review mechanisms to maintain credibility and trust.
Our Foundation

Four Strategic Pillars of CTIC

Building a resilient framework for global cybersecurity operations through standards, certification, research, and collaboration.

PILLAR 1

Standards & Framework Development

We contribute to the development of structured frameworks that guide threat intelligence lifecycle management, operational security processes, and forensic methodologies. These frameworks define:

  • Professional role expectations
  • Domain weightage and competencies
  • Ethical standards of practice
  • Continuing education pathways
PILLAR 2

Professional Certification

CTIC offers performance-based certifications that validate professional competency. Certification is one component of our broader mission to formalize professional standards within the industry. Our programs are:

  • Vendor-neutral
  • Blueprint-driven
  • Governance-reviewed
  • Designed to assess applied competence
PILLAR 3

Research & Publications

We publish threat briefings, whitepapers, analytical reports, and industry insights to promote knowledge exchange and evidence-based practices. CTIC supports:

  • Whitepapers
  • Analytical publications
  • Threat landscape reports
  • Academic collaboration initiatives
PILLAR 4

Global Collaboration

CTIC engages with academic institutions, enterprises, and cybersecurity professionals worldwide to foster collaboration and shared intelligence development.
